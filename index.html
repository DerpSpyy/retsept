Simple Mac and Cheese Recipe

  • Boil the macaroni until it's al dente.
  • Prepare a cheese sauce using butter, flour, milk, and cheese.
  • Combine macaroni and cheese sauce.
  • Bake in the oven for 25 minutes at 350Â°F.
  • Let cool, then serve and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macaroni
  • 2 cups grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
